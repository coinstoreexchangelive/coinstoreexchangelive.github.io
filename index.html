About Coinstore Exchange

Coinstore Exchange is designed for traders of all levels who want a reliable, transparent, and accessible platform to buy, sell, and manage cryptocurrency. Our platform supports major coins like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH) as well as hundreds of altcoins, providing deep liquidity, responsive customer support, and enterprise-grade security. Whether you’re executing your first trade or monitoring an institutional portfolio, Coinstore delivers the tools and performance you need.

Secure Wallet & Custody

We employ cold storage, multi-signature wallets, and regular security audits. Your funds are protected with industry-standard practices and insurance where available.

Low Fees & Transparent Pricing

Competitive maker/taker fees, volume discounts, and transparent fee schedules so you always know what you pay. No hidden charges.

Advanced Trading Tools

Real-time charts, limit and market orders, stop-loss and take-profit, plus API access for algorithmic traders and bots.

How Coinstore Works

Getting started is quick: create an account, complete identity verification for KYC-compliant features, and deposit funds using bank transfers, credit/debit, or crypto deposits. Use the simple Buy/Sell interface for instant orders, or switch to Advanced Trading to access order books and historical charts. Withdrawals are processed with multiple verification steps to keep assets safe.

Trading Pairs & Markets

Coinstore lists a variety of trading pairs including BTC/USD, ETH/USD, BTC/ETH and many altcoin pairs. We prioritize liquidity and carefully review all token listings for regulatory and technical compliance.

Security & Compliance

Security is central: two-factor authentication (2FA), withdrawal whitelists, IP/session management, and encryption at rest and in transit. We comply with local regulations and maintain robust KYC/AML programs to protect the ecosystem.

Fees & Limits

Fee tiers are based on 30-day trading volume. Small traders benefit from low entry-level fees while high-volume traders and market makers receive discounted rates. Deposit methods may have separate processing fees. Check the Fees page for a detailed breakdown.
