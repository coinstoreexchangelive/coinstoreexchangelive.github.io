Navigating DeFi & Altcoins Safely

While exploring new tokens and DeFi projects, always perform due diligence. Verify token contract addresses, check project audits, and be cautious with permissioned smart contracts. Coinstore lists tokens after security review, but users should remain informed and avoid sharing private keys or seed phrases.

Security & Compliance

Security is central to Coinstore’s operations. We use multi-layered protections including cold storage for the majority of holdings, encrypted key management, regular security audits, and optional withdrawal whitelists. Where applicable, users may be required to complete KYC (Know Your Customer) to access full platform features. Always enable 2FA and use strong, unique passwords for your account.
